Data Analytics

Data analytics is becoming increasingly important in today's data-driven world. Whether you are informing decision-makers on how to proceed with a business decision or persuading consumers to buy a product, data analytics is essential. So, where can you access data? Typically, in spreadsheets, relational databases, and the Internet/Internet of Things (IoT).

Once the data is gathered, there are a number of tools that can create analyses. Tableau Software is a highly visual tool. Microsoft Power BI is another tool. Google Analytics gives businesses the ability to quickly find out important information about users to their websites. Creating dashboards with these tools requires a strong attention to detail.
