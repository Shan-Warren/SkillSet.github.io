Finance and Investing

Financial modeling is a complex process that requires understanding of some key core concepts.

When valuing a company, it is important to remember that companies tend to grow at a slower rate as they increase in size.

Financial modeling is important for investing in the stock market, advising on mergers and acquisitions and acquiring companies in leveraged buyouts.

Financial modeling is only one part to the story of making a financial decision.
